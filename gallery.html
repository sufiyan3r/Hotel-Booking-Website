We at Markhor Hotels strongly believe in encouraging the quality of life of each of our guests. Keeping that in mind, we offer you a spacious gym to stay fit and active even while on vacation, reinvigorating treatments at the spa, or the temperature-controlled pool to let all your worries fade away.

Take advantage of 650 m2 of space that’s large enough to make you feel like you’re working out on your own. What’s more, we have an ultramodern Technogym Biocircuit that allows you to input your biometrics and the systems create a personalised workout just for you.
Whether you wish to work on your sun-kissed tan or just fancy a refreshing dip in the water overlooking Downtown Dubai, our rooftop pool is just the place for you.
